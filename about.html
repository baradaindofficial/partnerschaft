Barada Kanta Satpathy

My career spans 19 years across retail execution, procurement operations, and BTL marketing governance — in consumer electronics, FMCG, and services. The Business Model

How the Mediator
Model Works.

No setup costs passed to you. No bloated team. Just expertise and a network that delivers.

1

You share a brief

Service area, target locations, timeline, budget — the more context, the faster we move. One form, one call.

2

We identify the right vendor

Our pan-India pre-vetted network is mapped against your requirement. City, category, scale — matched in 24–48 hours.

We coordinate everything
